Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data that DC Bot collects, how we use that data, and the options our users have regarding their data.

Data Collection

DC Bot collects the following types of data:

  • User ID: This is used to identify users and provide personalized features.
  • Guild ID: This is used to identify the servers where DC Bot is used.

Data Usage

The data collected by DC Bot is used to provide and improve the features of the bot. We do not share this data with any third parties.

Data Protection

We take user privacy seriously and implement measures to protect user data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

User Rights

Users have the right to delete their data. If a user wants to exercise these rights, they can contact us through the contact information provided below.

Data Deletion

When DC Bot is removed from a server, all data associated with that server is automatically deleted.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at dsc.gg/d-community or DM DC Bot and we will get in touch with the user.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

This policy is effective as of 01/06/2024.
